Alpro

2022

During my Master's program in UX/UI Design, I had the opportunity to work on a website redesign project for Alpro.it.

The problem

The main challenge was the separation of the website's shop and main site, which resulted in two different interfaces, creating confusion and discouraging users from making purchases.

The goal

To address this issue, I unified the shop and main site, and redesigned the website's UI, improving the layout of information, the use of colors and fonts, and the visibility of images. I also enhanced the search filters and added new features, such as a live chat and review section.

The process

  • Heuristic analysis, benchmarking, and SWOT analysis
  • User research conducted through surveys, drafting of three personas, and definition of user journey and user flow
  • Reorganized the site map and content architecture
  • Definition of Wireframes, Design system and Prototype using Figma software, both for the Mobile and Desktop versions.

User Testing

I have decided to use a remotely conducted Moderate usability test to obtain predominantly qualitative data. The test was conducted on 3 participants selected through a screening survey. Once the script was prepared, I interviewed the participants via the Zoom platform to observe their reactions and collect data based on body language. After each interview, I conducted a debriefing to analyze the findings and identify Pain Points, which I then classified according to severity level to prioritize redesign efforts.
